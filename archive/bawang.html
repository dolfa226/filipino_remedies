Bawang

English Name: Garlic

Scientific Name: Allium sativum

Fever
Bawang Tsaa
Garlic Tea

Description

Bawang’s warm properties cause your body to sweat which lowers a high fever.

Smell

Pungent

Remedy

Drink

Time Duration

10 minutes

Preparation

  1. Boil bawang leaves and bulbs in water
  2. Allow it cool down to lukewarm temperature
  3. Apply to the head and body

High Cholesterol
Katas ng Bawang
Bitter Melon Tea

Description

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) under the Department of Health (DOH of the Philippines has approved bawang as an anti-cholesterol for therapeutic use. Bawang is used for conditions associated with the heart and circulatory system.

Remedy

Drink

Time Duration

5 minutes

Taste

Pungent

Preparation

  1. Take 1000mg to 7200mg of aged garlic extract in divided doses daily for at least 4 months

Tableta ng Bawang
Garlic Tablets

Description

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) under the Department of Health (DOH of the Philippines has approved bawang as an anti-cholesterol for therapeutic use. Bawang is used for conditions associated with the heart and circulatory system.

Remedy

Tablet

Time Duration

5 minutes

Taste

Neutral or bitter

Preparation

  1. Garlic powder tablet: 600mg to 900mg in divided doses daily for at least 6 weeks
  2. Other garlic powder products: take 300mg twice daily for at least 12 weeks

Skin
Katas ng Bawang
Garlic Extract

Description

Use the extract of the bawang to disinfect a wound.

Remedy

Application

Time Duration

15 minutes

Smell

Pungent

Preparation

  1. Crush and juice the garlic bulb
  2. Apply onto affected area
  3. Cover the afflicted area with a gauze and bandage
