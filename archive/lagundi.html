Lagundi

Scientific Name: Vitex negundo

Respiratory
Langundi Tsaa
Lagundi Tea

Description

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) under the Department of Health (DOH of the Philippines has approved the therapeutic use of lagundi for cough and asthma. Lagundi is widely recognized for treating cough and asthma symptoms.

Remedy

Drink

Time

25 minutes

Taste

Bitter, pungent, astringent

Preparation

  1. Boil at least half a cup of Lagundi leaves in 2 cups of water
  2. Drain the liquid
  3. Drink ½ a cup of the decoction 3 times a day
  4. For prolonged efficiency, pour boiling water over the Lagundi leaves and let the leaves soak
