Fever

Bawang Tsaa
Garlic Tea

Description

Bawang’s warm properties cause your body to sweat which lowers a high fever.

Smell

Pungent

Remedy

Drink

Time Duration

10 minutes

Preparation

  1. Boil bawang leaves and bulbs in water
  2. Allow it cool down to lukewarm temperature
  3. Apply to the head and body

Sambong Tapalan
Sambong Poultice

Description

Sambong is mashed together to form a poultice for the head and underarms to lower a fever.

Smell

Menthol

Remedy

Application

Time Duration

10 minutes

Preparation

  1. Crush leaves and soak in cold water
  2. Twist and squeeze water out and place between sheets of clean cloth
  3. Place on forehead or armpit to lower body temperature
