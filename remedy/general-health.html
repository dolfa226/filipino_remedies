Tsaang Gubat Tsaa
Tsaang Gubat Tea

Description

Tsaang gubat was used to improve general health in traditional medicine and used widely throughout the Philippines.

Remedy

Tea

Time

25 minutes

Preparation

  1. Wash the leaves of tsaang gubat in running water
  2. Chop into desirable size
  3. Boil every one cup of chopped leaves to 2 cups of water
  4. Boil every one cup of chopped leaves to 2 cups of water
  5. Boil at low heat for 15 to 20 minutes
  6. Strain and drink one cup per day
