Gout

The inflammation of joints

Ulasimang Bato Tsaa
Peperomia Tea

Description

Ulasimang Bato’s anti-inflammatory properties treat gout (the inflammation of joints).

Remedy

Tea

Time

10 minutes

Taste

Mild Pepper

Preparation

  1. Wash thoroughly 1 cup of the stalk and leaves of ulasimang bato
  2. Boil in 2 cups of water
  3. Strain and drink one cup in the morning and one cup in the evening
